Full-Body Strength Workout

Duration: 4 weeks | Difficulty: Beginner

Week 1

  • Squats: 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets x 8 reps
  • Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets x 20 seconds

Week 2

  • Lunges: 3 sets x 12 reps (each leg)
  • Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets x 8 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets x 12 reps (each side)

Week 3

  • Step-Ups: 3 sets x 12 reps (each leg)
  • Push-Ups with Rotation: 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 3 sets x 8 reps
  • Plank with Leg Lifts: 3 sets x 12 reps (each leg)

Bodyweight Strength Workout

Duration: 8 weeks | Difficulty: Advanced

Week 1

  • Push-Ups: 4 sets x 15 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Pistol Squats: 4 sets x 8 reps (each leg)
  • Plank: 4 sets x 30 seconds

Week 2

  • Push-Ups: 4 sets x 15 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Pistol Squats: 4 sets x 8 reps (each leg)
  • Plank: 4 sets x 30 seconds

Week 3

  • Diamond Push-Ups: 4 sets x 15 reps
  • Chin-Ups: 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Single-Leg Glute Bridge: 4 sets x 10 reps (each leg)
  • Side Plank: 4 sets x 30 seconds (each side)

Isolated Muscle Strength Workout

Duration: 6 weeks | Difficulty: Intermediate

Week 1

  • Legs (Quads): Leg Extensions - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Chest: Bench Press - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Back (Lats): Lat Pulldowns - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Shoulders (Delts): Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 3 sets x 10 reps

Week 2

  • Legs (Quads): Leg Press - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Chest: Dumbbell Flyes - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Back (Lats): Seated Cable Rows - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Shoulders (Delts): Lateral Raises - 3 sets x 12 reps

Week 3

  • Legs (Hamstrings): Romanian Deadlifts - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Chest: Incline Bench Press - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Back (Rhomboids): Face Pulls - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Shoulders (Delts): Front Raises - 3 sets x 12 reps
