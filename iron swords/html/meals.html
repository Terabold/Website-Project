Explore our meal plans to support your fitness goals. Choose between different meal plan options tailored to your needs.

Protein-Packed Meal Plan

Duration: 4 weeks | Goal: Muscle Gain

Week 1

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and roasted vegetables.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with berries and a drizzle of honey.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with brown rice and steamed broccoli.

Week 2

  • Breakfast: Protein smoothie with banana, whey protein, and almond milk.
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with a side salad.
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple and nuts.
  • Dinner: Grilled lean steak with sweet potatoes and asparagus.

Week 3

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with protein powder, berries, and almond butter.
  • Lunch: Quinoa bowl with black beans, vegetables, and grilled shrimp.
  • Snack: Protein bar and a small apple.
  • Dinner: Baked chicken with quinoa and mixed greens salad.

Keto Meal Plan

Duration: 8 weeks | Goal: Ketosis and Fat Loss

  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and olive oil dressing.
  • Snack: Sliced cucumbers with cream cheese.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with broccoli and garlic butter.

Week 2

  • Breakfast: Keto-friendly smoothie with almond milk, spinach, and protein powder.
  • Lunch: Zucchini noodles with pesto and cherry tomatoes.
  • Snack: Almonds and a small serving of blueberries.
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with cauliflower mash and asparagus.

Week 3

  • Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with coconut milk and raspberries.
  • Lunch: Sliced turkey and cheese roll-ups with a side of olives.
  • Snack: Keto-friendly protein bar and celery sticks.
  • Dinner: Baked cod with sautéed spinach and lemon-garlic sauce.

Vegetarian Meal Plan
