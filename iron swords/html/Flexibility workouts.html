Flexibility and Mobility Exercises

Improve your flexibility and mobility with these effective exercises and stretches.

Types of Stretching

Explore different types of stretching exercises to enhance your flexibility and range of motion.

Dynamic Stretching

  • Leg Swings: Stand next to a wall and swing one leg forward and backward.
  • Arm Circles: Extend your arms and make circular motions with your wrists and shoulders.
  • Lunges with a Twist: Lunge forward and twist your upper body to the side of the leading leg.

Static Stretching

  • Hamstring Stretch: Sit with one leg extended, reach for your toes, and hold the stretch.
  • Quad Stretch: Stand on one leg, grab your opposite ankle, and pull it towards your glutes.
  • Triceps Stretch: Extend one arm overhead and bend your elbow, using the opposite hand to push gently on the bent elbow.

PNF Stretching

  • Hamstring PNF Stretch: Lie on your back, raise one leg, and have a partner gently push your leg towards your head while you resist.
  • Quad PNF Stretch: Lie face down, bend your knee, and have a partner push your foot towards your glutes while you resist.
  • Shoulder PNF Stretch: Hold one arm straight out, and have a partner push it towards your chest while you resist.

Mobility Exercises

Enhance your joint mobility and reduce stiffness with these mobility drills.

Hip Circles

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and make circular motions with your hips.
  • Repeat the motion in the opposite direction.

Shoulder Rotations

  • Stand or sit with your arms at your sides, and make slow, controlled circles with your shoulders.
  • Switch directions and repeat.

Ankle Circles

  • Sit on the floor with your legs extended, and rotate your ankles in circular motions.
  • Perform ankle circles in both clockwise and counterclockwise directions.

Flexibility Workouts

Combine different stretches and mobility exercises into comprehensive flexibility workouts.

Full-Body Flexibility Routine

  • Leg Swings: 2 sets of 15 swings per leg
  • Quad Stretch: 3 sets of 20 seconds per leg
  • Shoulder Rotations: 2 sets of 10 rotations per direction

Morning Mobility Routine

  • Hip Circles: 2 sets of 10 circles per direction
  • Arm Circles: 2 sets of 15 circles per direction
  • Ankle Circles: 2 sets of 15 circles per direction
